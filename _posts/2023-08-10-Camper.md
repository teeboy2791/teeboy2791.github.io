## New Camper

We ordered this before we knew I would have a career break, but the delivery timing was perfect!

The spec is:

- Van 150 Ocean Auto DSG
- Cherry Red - Paint
- Palladium - Interior
- 17"x 7J "Aracju" Silver alloy wheels (Tyres 215/60 R17 C 109/107T)
- Acoustic package with heated windscreen 
- Towbar - removable & lockable (incl. trailer stabilisation) 
- Cupboards in "Graphite Grey" with outdoor shower connection 
- High-beam control 
- All-season tires 215/60 R17 C 109/107T 104H, super-low rolling resistance
- Mudflaps front and rear
- Power sliding door on the right in load/passenger compartment 
- Safe Box
- Discover Pro navigation system including ‘Streaming & Internet’
